# AI-Powered Interview Assistant

## 🎯 Overview

The AI-Powered Interview Assistant is an intelligent web application designed to help job seekers **assess their resumes and practice interviews**. By leveraging AI, users can receive **resume feedback, personalized scores, and simulated interviews**, enhancing their preparation for real-world job opportunities.

---

## ⚙️ Features

- **Resume Assessment:** Upload your resume, select your target role, and receive a **comprehensive score**.  
- **Detailed Feedback:** View **summary, strengths, and weaknesses** to understand areas for improvement.  
- **Interview Simulator:** Generate **role-specific interview questions** and practice answering them one by one.  
- **Real-Time Feedback:** Get **constructive evaluation** after each answer to improve performance.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** Streamlit – interactive and user-friendly interface  
- **Backend:** Python – core logic and AI integration  
- **AI Model:** Gemini API – generates interview questions and evaluates answers  
- **NLP & Embeddings:** For semantic understanding of resumes and questions  
- **PDF Handling:** PyPDF2 – extract text from resumes for analysis

## 🔄 Step-by-Step Workflow

---

### 🪪 Step 1: Resume Upload & Scoring

1. Upload your resume (PDF format).  
2. Select your **target job role** (e.g., Data Analyst, Web Developer, AI Engineer).  
3. The AI evaluates your resume and provides a **comprehensive score** based on:
   - Skill relevance  
   - Experience alignment  
   - Keyword optimization  

📊 **Output:**  
Get a detailed **resume score breakdown** showing your alignment with the selected job role.

### 🧾 Step 2: Resume Feedback

1. The AI generates a **summary of your profile**.  
2. View **strengths and weaknesses**, highlighting:
   - What stands out in your resume  
   - What can be improved for better results  
3. Use the insights to refine your resume and increase your interview chances.

🗒️ **Example Output:**
- ✅ **Strengths:** Strong technical projects, clear skill presentation  
- ⚠️ **Weaknesses:** Missing measurable outcomes, lack of domain keywords

### 🎯 Step 3: Interview Simulator

1. Select your target role (e.g., Software Engineer, Data Scientist, etc.).  
2. The AI generates **domain-specific interview questions**.  
3. Begin the **mock interview** — answer questions one by one.  
4. After each response, receive **real-time AI feedback** on:
   - Relevance  
   - Confidence  
   - Clarity  
   - Technical accuracy  

🎙️ **Goal:** Improve your answering skills and communication with instant evaluation.
